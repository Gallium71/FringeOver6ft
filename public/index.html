About Fringe Over 6ft
A community project built by tall people, for tall people.
🪑 What is this?

Fringe Over 6ft is a crowdsourced database of seating comfort at Edinburgh Fringe and International Festival venues.

We cover 70+ venues and 200+ individual spaces.

📐 How scoring works

Reviews use a 0–5 chair scale, Gaussian-weighted by height (σ=15cm). Set your height for personalised scores.

📸 Verified reviews (ticket stub approved by our team) count 5× more.

🗜️ The Awards

Winners decided entirely by your reviews. No judges. We hand out the plastic tat prestigious trophies at season end.

✍️ Contributing
  • Register and set your height
  • Rate legroom honestly (0–5)
  • Add a sentence on seat type, row tips
  • 📸 Upload ticket stub for 5× weight
💰 How we stay free

Occasional clearly-marked travel/accommodation links. They never affect scores. No venue can pay for a better rating. Ever.
